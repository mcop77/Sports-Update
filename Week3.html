Swimming

Boys Swim Team State Runner up....Boys won the 200M medley relay, three seconds under the old team record. Gabe Parnell just missed winning the 200M, got touched out on the end but had one of the fastest times in the nation. He then came back and won the hundred breaststroke.


Cole Parnell and Robert Frankenstein finaled in the 50M free. Jhon Moncada made finals in the 100M back. Cameron Claytor, Jhon Moncada, and Cole Parnell made consolation finals in their other event. 200M free relay was a very fast fourth place and we finished the meet second in the 400M free relay with a team record, beating the previous time by five seconds.


The girls all swam well at finals in both of their relays. Shannon Hagmeir finaled in the 50M free and 100M breast and Julia Sherlock finaled in the 100M back, all having their best times.



Powder Puff

The Seniors played the Juniors on Wednesday, crushing them 14 - 0. Good try Juniors, better luck next year!
