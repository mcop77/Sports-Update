Fall Sports Recap and what's coming up this week! (30 October - 3 November, 2017)







FALL SPORTS RECAP


Bowling

Boys Varsity Bowling had a tough season, winning only 2 out of their 16 matches. Girls Varsity Bowling had better luck, going 5-9-0 and crushed Lyman 1697-1022. Everyone did a great job!



Football

Varsity Football is still playing. JV Football had a great season, going 5-2, and also beat Lyman 42-6 on their last game. Freshman Boys had an amazing season, going 7-1, only losing at Lake Mary to the Rams. Overall a great season of Football at Lake Brantley.



Golf

Boys Golf had a great season, with Ian Cameron and Ryan Carey advancing to regions. Girls Golf had many cancelations because of weather, but Paige Anderson was still able to advance to Regionals. We are definitely looking forward to next season!



Slow Pitch Softball

Slow Pitch Softball had a tough season, going 4-10. Despite this, the girls still were able to come out with outstanding wins against Seminole and Lake Howell, winning 14-0 and 13-0. Great job ladies!



Girls Volleyball

Girls Varsity Volleyball came away with 10-8 record, winning 2 games without the other team even getting a set. JV Volleyball had a tougher season, winning 3 out of their 14 games. The freshman girls however, did a fantastic job, going 9-4, crushing their opponents. Great job girls, we're looking forward to the next season!



Cross Country

Boys and Girls Varsity Cross Country competed in the District meet @ Seminole State College this past Saturday. The Girls placed 2nd overall, and the Boys placed 5th. Both will advance to Regionals at the Flagler County Fairgrounds this Saturday. Good Luck!
