Last Week's Recap(10/30 - 11/3)



Cross Country

Boys Cross Country placed 5th at districts and the Girls placed 2nd. They both teams move on to regionals. Girls Varsity did great, with Ashley Klingenberg finishing 3rd and Chloe Hyde finishing 9th, allowing them to move on to States. Boys Varsity also did well, with Juan Moscosco placing 7th overall.



Weightlifting

Girls Weightlifting had a meet against Lyman, but lost 38 - 49.



Swimming

Boys Swimming did a great job, with Gabe Parnell winning the 200 individual medley. Also the boys relay team of Gabe Parnell, Cole Parnell, Robert Frankenstein, and Jhon Moncada broke the school record in the 400 freestyle relay.



Football

Varsity Football played a tough game against Osceola, losing 8 - 25 in their final game of the season.
